Tadao Ando was born in Osaka in 1941.
He has studied architecture by himself and established Tadao Ando Architect & Associates in 1969. however, he was an honorary member of the American Institute of Architects, the American Academy of Arts and Letters, as well as the Royal Academy of Arts in London. He was also a visiting professor at Yale, Columbia, UC Berkeley, and Harvard Universities.
